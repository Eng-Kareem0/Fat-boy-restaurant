Our Story

Discover the tradition behind our authentic Italian pizzas

Since 1985

Authentic Italian Pizza Since 1985

Fat Boy Pizza was founded by Antonio Rossi who brought his family's secret pizza recipes from Naples to share with the world. What started as a small family-run pizzeria has grown into a beloved local institution.

We still use the same traditional methods and ingredients imported directly from Italy to create our authentic Neapolitan-style pizzas. Our wood-fired oven reaches temperatures of 900°F, creating the perfect crispy yet chewy crust in just 90 seconds.

Every pizza is handcrafted with care, using only the freshest ingredients and topped with love. We invite you to taste the difference that tradition and passion make.
